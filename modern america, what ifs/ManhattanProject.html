Since the question we are trying to answer is about atomic bombs. We need to learn about what the Manhattan Project was and what it did.

What was the Manhattan Project?

The Manhattan Project was started because two German scientists, Otto Hahn and Fritz Strassmann discovered nuclear fission which is the driving force of atomic bombs. A few months later, two American scientists Albert Einstein and Leo Szilard sent a letter to President Roosevelt, telling him that Germany could be trying to make an atomic bomb. So what was the Manhattan project for, you may ask? Well... I am going to answer that for you. The Manhatten project was created to make atomic bombs also in response to this information.
